Artois Probability
Gut Agency
Grand Prix — Creative Data 2× Gold — Outdoor & Print 2× Silver — Brand Experience & Activation, Creative Data

The history of Stella Artois can be traced back to the year 1366. From that time until now, there were lots of paintings in the history of art with beer portrayed in them. We calculated the probability of the beer portrayed in all those paintings to know if they could be, in fact, Stella Artois.

We crossed the data gathered from our original brewery and the history of art using the knowledge of mathematicians and historians from the Bellas Artes Museum. The probability was calculated based on variables such as the type of containers, the golden hue of the beer, potential competitors in the area, the year of creation, and the intersection between geographical location and the distribution of our original brewery.

We hosted all this data into an app that displayed the Artois Probability while scanning the beer on more than +300 paintings and counting.
