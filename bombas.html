Bombas

Bombas may have started as the internet's favorite sock company, but today, they're so much more. This year, we partnered with them to bring their refreshed brand expression to life. The goal was to shift the story from "the best socks on the internet" to "the comfort brand you can't live without."

For the rebrand, we built a visual system that feels playful, optimistic, and full of energy. A system that reflects their joy for impact and their belief that change can be driven with creativity and collaboration.

The identity is designed to feel human and approachable, bringing the brand closer to its community, partners, and students. Just as Learning by Helping generates social impact through meaningful action, the new identity seeks to create emotional impact through a bold, vibrant, and inviting expression.
