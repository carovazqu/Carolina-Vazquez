Elppa
Gut Agency

AB InBev wanted to launch their first cider in Korea. The challenge: build a brand from zero, from naming and branding to packaging, product testing, and campaign activation. Creating a brand from scratch is a challenge. But what if we could flip perspectives… literally? That's how ELPPA was born, a Korean apple cider with a bold, unexpected identity.

From naming to concept, we infused the brand with a twist: the ambigram that turns APPLE into ELPPA, the concept Turn Your Day Around, and a disruptive, energetic personality. More than a tagline, it's an invitation to flip perspectives and add a bold twist to everyday moments.
